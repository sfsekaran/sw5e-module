# 1.2.9-1.3.0 Beta Test List

Use this as a beta checklist. `1.3.0` includes a lot of `1.2.9` behavior, so testers on `1.3.0` should still re-test the starship and powercasting areas from `1.2.9`.

## 1.2.9 Beta Test List

- Legacy world conversion tool: import or convert an older SW5E world and verify actors, items, scenes, and compendium references survive the conversion.
- Vehicle-backed starship sheets: open multiple starships from compendiums and world actors and confirm the custom starship sheet renders without warnings or broken tabs.
- Starship tab behavior: verify the custom starship tabs open correctly and do not interfere with stock tabs.
- Starship skill rolls: roll several ship skills and confirm totals, roll dialogs, and chat output behave correctly.
- Starship travel display: confirm travel pace, hyperdrive class, and related summary cards show expected values.
- Starship movement runtime: verify flying speed, turning speed, and routing-based speed changes update correctly.
- Crew-aware starship summaries: assign or remove pilot/crew/passengers and confirm crew counts and summary text update correctly.
- Starship item quick actions: open, use, delete, and focus starship items from the sheet and confirm each action targets the correct item.
- Ship weapon interactions: test starship weapon attacks and damage, especially when routing affects weapons.
- Warning dialog handling: open known-problem ships and confirm the warnings dialog appears only when appropriate and does not break the sheet.
- Force and Tech point sheet workflow: verify points show correctly on character and NPC sheets and fit the dnd5e sheet flow.
- Powercasting display regressions: confirm power bars and sheet displays render correctly on both PCs and NPCs.
- Medpac workflow: test medpac-related actions that previously had syntax/runtime issues.
- Reload-related item workflows: test reload/ammo behavior on affected items.
- General V13 / `dnd5e` `5.2.5` compatibility: open sheets, roll items, and use activities that were previously affected by deprecated APIs.

## 1.3.0 Beta Test List

- Optional SW currencies: enable and disable currencies in module settings and confirm only enabled denominations appear in wallets and selectors.
- Currency settings app: verify it appears under `SW5E`, opens correctly, scrolls correctly, and saves changes.
- Custom exchange rates: configure fixed and GM-entered exchange rates and confirm they persist and display correctly.
- Currency tooltips: hover currency labels/fields and confirm tooltip text is fully resolved and accurate.
- Actor wallet rendering: confirm enabled SW currencies appear on the Inventory tab for characters and NPCs.
- Item price denomination selectors: verify item prices can use the enabled SW denominations without broken labels or missing values.
- Currency convert/transfer compatibility: test dnd5e convert and transfer actions with SW currencies on existing and newly created actors.
- Wallet normalization on existing actors: open older actors after update and verify their currency data is still valid and usable.
- HGTTG compendiums: open `HGTTG Species` and `HGTTG Species Features` and verify entries load, art displays, and data is usable in the V13 / `dnd5e` `5.2.5` workflow.
- HGTTG separation from core species: confirm HGTTG content is not mixed into the main `Species` compendium.
- Force and Tech point editing: on characters and NPCs, edit current points, save, press Enter, and confirm values do not drift, reset, or repeatedly subtract bonuses.
- Power point edit access: confirm the cog-only edit control appears where intended and works on both PCs and NPCs.
- Removed Power Point Controls panel: confirm the old redundant panel no longer appears on actor sheets.
- Image migration: open actors and vehicles with missing or legacy art and confirm they no longer default to the loot bag icon.
- Missing-image regression: verify stale `dnd5e` image references no longer produce repeated missing-image errors.
- Vehicle-backed starship migration: open legacy and migrated starships and confirm they now behave as vehicle-backed sheets.
- Starship data retention: verify migrated starships preserve movement, travel, crew, routing, and `details.type`.
- Starship compendium builds: pull test ships from compendiums, especially the B-wing, and confirm they import/open with correct ship data.
- Starship tab navigation: confirm the visible tab order is `SotG`, `SotG Features`, `Cargo`, `Effects`, `Description`.
- Stock Features tab removal on starships: confirm the stock `Features` tab is hidden only on SW5E starship sheets and the remaining tabs use the full width.

## High-Priority Regression Areas

- Starships opened from old worlds.
- The `A/SF-01 B-wing starfighter`.
- NPC Force/Tech point editing.
- Existing actors with pre-`1.3.0` currency wallets.
- Actors or vehicles that previously had missing avatars.

## What Testers Should Report

- World type: new world or migrated/older world.
- Document source: world actor, imported actor, or compendium actor.
- Whether the issue is GM-only or also affects players.
- Exact actor, item, or starship name.
- Whether the problem is visual only, data-saving, or chat/roll related.
